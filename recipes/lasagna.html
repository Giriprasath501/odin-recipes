Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese. It is hearty, cheesy, and perfect for family dinners or celebrations.

This version of lasagna is easy to make, incredibly flavorful, and guaranteed to impress your guests with its layers of deliciousness!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 500g ground beef
  • 2 cups tomato sauce
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, sauté onion and garlic. Add ground beef and cook until browned.
  4. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer noodles, ricotta cheese, meat sauce, and mozzarella. Repeat layers.
  7. Top with mozzarella and parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 25–30 minutes until cheese is melted and golden.
  9. Let rest for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
